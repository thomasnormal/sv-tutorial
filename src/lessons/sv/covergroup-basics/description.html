Functional coverage answers a different question than assertions: not "did something illegal happen?" but "did we hit the scenarios we care about?".

A covergroup defines what to measure, and a coverpoint tracks sampled values:

covergroup cg @(posedge clk);
  coverpoint opcode;
endgroup

Open cov_intro.sv and add the missing coverpoint line for opcode.

Coverage is only meaningful if the testbench drives interesting stimulus. A 100% score on weak stimulus can still miss real bugs.
