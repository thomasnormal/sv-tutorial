SVA lets you compose two sequences side-by-side. The key operators:

  • s1 intersect s2 — both must match and end at exactly the same cycle (lock-step)
  • s1 and s2 — both must match, but may end at different cycles (independent)

Open bus_check.sv and complete two properties:

  1. p_transfer: once valid is asserted, both valid and ready must hold high for exactly 4 consecutive cycles in lock-step.
  2. p_complete: after valid, ready must arrive within 4 cycles and done must arrive within 8 cycles — checked independently.

Two related operators: or (at least one sequence must match) and within (one sequence must start and end entirely inside another's window). Use those when you need "either/or" or containment checks.
