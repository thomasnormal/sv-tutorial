The throughout operator requires an expression to hold true at every clock tick for the entire duration of a sequence:

expr throughout seq

At each evaluation step of seq, expr is checked. If expr ever goes false while seq is still running, the sequence fails.

A typical use is mode/enable stability: chip-select must stay asserted for an entire 8-clock SPI transfer. Once cs_n falls (goes active-low), it must not rise again for the whole transfer — 8 consecutive MOSI bits.

Open spi_check.sv and complete the property: when cs_n falls, use throughout to assert that cs_n stays low for an 8-cycle MOSI sequence.

(!cs_n) throughout seq is equivalent to writing sync_reject_on(cs_n) seq — both fail as soon as cs_n rises mid-transfer. throughout is usually more readable for the stability idiom.
