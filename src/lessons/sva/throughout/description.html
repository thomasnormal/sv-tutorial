The throughout operator requires an expression to hold true at every clock tick for the entire duration of a sequence:

expr throughout seq

At each evaluation step of seq, expr is checked. If expr ever goes false while seq is still running, the sequence fails.

A typical use is mode/enable stability: chip-select must stay asserted for an entire 8-clock SPI transfer:

$fell(cs_n) |=> (!cs_n) throughout (mosi[*8])
